How to Use the Sales Tax Calculator

Calculate sales tax quickly with our free online calculator. Add tax to prices or remove tax from totals.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Enter Price: Input the price amount in dollars
  2. Enter Tax Rate: Input the sales tax rate as a percentage
  3. Select Type: Choose to add tax to price or remove tax from total
  4. Click Calculate: Press the button to see results

Common Sales Tax Rates by State (US)

  • California: 7.25%
  • Texas: 6.25%
  • Florida: 6%
  • New York: 4%
  • Pennsylvania: 6%

Note: Local rates may vary. Check your local tax rate for accurate calculations.
