How to Use the Grade Calculator

Calculate your weighted average grade across multiple assignments, tests, and projects.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Enter Assignment Name: Optional name for each grade
  2. Enter Grade: The percentage score you received (0-100)
  3. Enter Weight: The percentage this grade counts toward your final grade
  4. Add More Grades: Click "+ Add Another Grade" to add more entries
  5. Calculate: Click to see your weighted final grade

Example

  • Homework: 85% (weight: 20%)
  • Midterm: 75% (weight: 30%)
  • Final Exam: 90% (weight: 50%)
  • Final Grade: 83.5%
