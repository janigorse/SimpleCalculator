Calculate your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth.

  2. Optional: Enter a specific date to calculate age as of that date (defaults to today)
  3. Click Calculate: See your exact age

Common Uses

  • Calculate exact age for official documents
  • Find out how old someone will be on a future date
  • Calculate age difference between two people
